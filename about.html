Who We Are

The mission of Nagalaale Foundation is to trigger and catalyze transformative self-led initiatives in education and sports, healthcare, and livelihoods within communities.

Nagalaale Foundation

Nagalaale Foundation is a community-oriented charity focused on catalyzing community self-led initiatives aimed at uplifting livelihood standards through maximizing the available resources. The charity achieves this through nurturing individual potential and triggering community endeavors for the common good. It is built on the African philosophy of communality, “I am because we are; and we are because I am”.

Nagalaale Foundation is headed by a Board of Trustees, who provide strategic direction and oversight to the Secretariat. The Secretariat in turn steers and oversees the operations of the Foundation’s major projects including the Community Health Center, a Vocational Secondary School, and the Nagalaale Country House. Each of these projects is guided by a committee comprised of community members that represent those that the project serves.

Our Objectives.

  • Establish and operate a community-led Health Center IV providing quality healthcare services innovatively
  • Establish and operate a vocational secondary school in the community
  • Establish and operate Nagalaale Country House as a springboard for the Foundation’s engagement in hospitality and community tourism
