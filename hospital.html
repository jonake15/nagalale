Nagalaale Community Health Center IV

1 / 3
2 / 3
3 / 3
❮ ❯

The Health Center is to be registered as a Private-Not-For Profit and envisaged to be the nucleus for transformative innovations in community-based healthcare services within the entire district of Wakiso and beyond. On top of providing the conventional services expected of a Health Center IV, this facility aspires to evolve into a learning hub including nursing and paramedical training, as well as an Academic Health Center, where interdisciplinary research happens. In this sense, the health center will position itself as a demonstration for the One-Health concept. Key areas of focus will be: (i) Promoting youth access to appropriate health services as provided by Government and other actors, particularly reproductive health and HIV/AIDS; (ii) Maternal healthcare and immunization; and (iii) Preventive healthcare (Primary healthcare). Nagalaale Foundation would like to use the Health Center as a tool for broadening and deepening community-based healthcare as an effective approach to enhancing livelihoods and economic productivity of communities. There is a direct co-relation between health and production.
